## Definitions

#### Image
- The result of multiplying by some matrix
- $\begin{bmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} x \\ y \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} ax + by \\ cx + dy \end{bmatrix} = $ Image

&nbsp;

## Derivations

#### Reflection in the y-axis

We want the point $(x, y)$ to become $(-x, y)$

$$
\begin{bmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} x\\ y \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} -x \\ y \end{bmatrix}
$$

by inspection we can see this matrix should be

$$
\begin{bmatrix} -1 & 0 \\ 0 & 1 \end{bmatrix}
$$

&nbsp;

#### Reflection in the x-axis

We want the point $(x, y)$ to become $(x, -y)$

$$
\begin{bmatrix} a & b \\ c & d \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} x\\ y \end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix} x \\ -y \end{bmatrix}
$$

by inspection we can see this matrix should be

$$
\begin{bmatrix} 1 & 0 \\ 0 & -1 \end{bmatrix}
$$
