Linear algebra is the study of vectors, matrices, and linear transformations. It provides a powerful framework for solving systems of equations and understanding geometric transformations.

## Overview

- **Fundamentals** - Vectors, matrices, and basic operations
- **Matrix Operations** - Multiplication, determinants, and inverses
- **Linear Transformations** - Mappings and their matrix representations
