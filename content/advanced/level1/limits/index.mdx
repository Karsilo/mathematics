# Limits

A limit describes the behavior of a function as its input approaches a particular value. Limits are foundational to calculus and analysis.

## Overview

- **Definition** - Formal and intuitive understanding
- **Types** - One-sided, infinite, and limits at infinity
- **Techniques** - Methods for evaluating limits
- **Applications** - Continuity, derivatives, and integrals
