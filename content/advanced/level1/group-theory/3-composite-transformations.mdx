### $sr = u$

$$
(1, 2, 3) \quad \mapsto \quad (1, 3, 2) \quad \mapsto \quad (2, 1, 3) \quad = \quad u
$$

&nbsp;

### $rs = t$

$$
(1, 2, 3) \quad \mapsto \quad (3, 1, 2) \quad \mapsto \quad (3, 2, 1) \quad = \quad t
$$

&nbsp;

### $r^2s = u = sr$

$$
(1, 2, 3) \quad \mapsto \quad (2, 3, 1) \quad \mapsto \quad (2, 1, 3) \quad = \quad u
$$

&nbsp;

### $sr^2 = t = rs$

$$
(1, 2, 3) \quad \mapsto \quad (1, 3, 2) \quad \mapsto \quad (3, 2, 1) \quad = \quad t
$$
