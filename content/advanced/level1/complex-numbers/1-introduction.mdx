### Definition

#### Imaginary Number
$$
i = \sqrt{-1}
$$

#### Complex Number
$$
4i,\;\;\; 3 + i,\;\;\; 8-4i
$$

&nbsp;

Complex numbers are all **real** & **imaginary** numbers or a combination of.


$$
z = a + b i, \quad a, b \in \mathbb{R}, \quad z \in \mathbb{C}
$$

&nbsp;


### Example

$$
\begin{aligned}
\text{a)} \quad & (3 + 2i) + (5 - 4i) &&= 8 - 2i \\[1mm]
\text{b)} \quad & (5 - 3i)(5 + 2i)     &&= 25 + 10i - 15i - 6i^2 \\
                &                       &&= 25 - 5i + 6 \\
                &                       &&= 31 - 5i
\end{aligned}
$$


### Notation

Let $ \; \; \; \; z = a + bi \; $

then $ \; Re(z) = a$

& $ \; \; \; \; \; Im(z) = b$
