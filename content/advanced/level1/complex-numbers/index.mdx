title: Complex Numbers
description: Study of numbers with real and imaginary parts
---

Complex numbers extend the real number system by including the imaginary unit $i$, where 

$$
i^2 = -1
$$

## Overview

- **Definition** - Form **a + bi** where **a** and **b** are real numbers
- **Representations** - Cartesian, polar, and exponential forms
- **Operations** - Arithmetic with complex numbers
- **Applications** - Engineering, physics, and signal processing
